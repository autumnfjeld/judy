About
CEO and founder of Kennedy Home Management, Judy Kennedy is a 3rd generation Phoenician who has dedicated her life to the betterment of the people of Phoenix, Arizona. Her family has a deep history in Arizona's construction industry: building bridges, roads, and homes, improving infrastructure, and providing jobs for the people of Arizona. House restoration has been Judy's career focus since 2009 and includes five successfully restored homes returned to the market within four months.
